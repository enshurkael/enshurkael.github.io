# Enshur-Kael

**Enshur-Kael** is an independent research archive focused on ancient medical theories, observational records, and unresolved questions in medicine.

## Scope
- Ancient medical systems
- Unexplained medical phenomena
- Theoretical frameworks beyond modern clinical models
- Comparative and historical analysis

## Nature of Content
- Academic and exploratory
- Non-commercial
- Non-clinical
- Observational and theoretical

## Sections
- **Theory** – conceptual and historical medical ideas
- **Observations** – structured field notes and experiential records
- **About** – purpose, ethics, and authorship position

## Disclaimer
This project does **not** provide medical advice.  
All content is for research and academic discussion only.
