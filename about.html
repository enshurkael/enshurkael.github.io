About This Project

Enshur-Kael is an independent intellectual project focused on ancient medical thought, theoretical exploration, and unanswered questions in medicine.

This platform is intended as a space for reflection, hypothesis, and long-term inquiry. It does not claim to provide treatment, diagnosis, or clinical conclusions.

Ideas published here may evolve over time as understanding deepens.

This project is academic and exploratory in nature.
